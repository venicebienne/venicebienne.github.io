18 Again and the Emotional Rollercoaster it Had Us On

One of the best dramas to come out last year, and our author's all time favorite Korean Drama, 18 Again, has some of the best characters, plot, and cinematography in KDrama history. The drama was produced by JTBC who is known for producing some of the highest rated Korean Dramas such as SKY Castle and The World of the Married. Just like these two groundbreaking shows, 18 Again definitely did not disappoint.

The main character, Hong Daeyeong, wishes to be 18 again which was the prime of his life. Sure enough, his wish became true and he now has to go by the alias Ko U-yeong as he enrolled to his children's high school to spend more time with them for he was a neglectful father pre-divorce. As he spends more time with his family as an 18 year-old, he slowly starts to fall in love again with his ex-wife, Dajeong. This causes all sorts of problems since his children and ex-wife do not know that he is Hong Daeyeong.

18 Again will give you all the feels! You will have lots of laughs, cries, and heartbreaks to experience as you watch through the 16-episode series. It has romance, family drama, slice of life, self-discovery, and some sort of fantasy. These alone make the drama such a good one, but what makes it the best is the cast and their skills. Lee Do Hyun, the actor who protrays Hong Daeyeong/Ko Uyeong, does a magnificenrt job in portraying his character as he makes you feel emotional every single scene. If you have time, defnitely give this drama a watch. We think it is highly underrated, and it is high time for it to become one of the most well-known shows out there!

The 5 Best Dramas to Watch Right Now

1. Yumi's Cells

The drama based from the webtoon of the same name is a must-watch! It features adorable characters that make the story 10x better than it already is.

2. Law School

As the 2022 Elections come closer, this might be a good watch for those who are evaluating the kind of leaders they want. The show features lawful, responsible, and truthful protagonists that bring justice to and for the victims.

3. Move to Heaven

Though oftentimes will make you cry buckets, this drama features different types of stories that wikll get you hooked and binging every single time.

4. Live On

Though we miss face-to-face classes, Live On shows us the things we are lucky not to experience in the online learning setup. Revolving around a group of students, the show does a great job of portraying teens and is definitely relatable.

5. Search WWW

In the modern world, we are slowly but surely starting to see women take over in different fields, and Search WWW gives us Girlboss Galore as it centers around three women who climb their way up in the I.T. field.
